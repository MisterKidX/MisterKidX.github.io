VoX

Race! VoX is a unique VR arcade game where you’ll try to out best yourself and your friends. Collect coins, drift and stay on track! If you worked with VR, guess you could spot the challenge here: How do you handle motion sickness and racing in VR? It’s hard, but doable. Wrapping google anti motion sickness SDK with unity scripts while also improving on that SDK proved to be a viable solution.
