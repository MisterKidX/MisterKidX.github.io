Party Cat

Swipe your way around the house and clean it all up!
Genre Platform Role Status Client
Hyper Casual, Puzzle Mobile (IOS) Level Designer Published (Removed From Store) Ouzo (Closed)

Play the Game


Overview

Just like Color Maze you have to swipe your avatar around to fill the screen. Only in this little game the level design was a bit harder because of the demand was to make the board smaller and the levels more visually appealing. This meant that the puzzle element was even further compromised. But in the end I used the same techniques to create challenging levels even on smaller scales.

Took longer though 😉
