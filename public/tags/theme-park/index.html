Instead of cages, a 120 acre of savannah, teeming with life. Role Status Client Technical Designer & Consulter Published Hataasiya Overview A zoo is to be rebuilt as natural 120 acre savannanah, in the heart of the Israeli Negev. This Safari is not only meant to display the animals as some kind of trophies, but to actualy educate the people about the importance of wildelife perservance and to create empathy with the animals. As part of this new park, set in Beer Sheva, are 18 unique interactive experiences and games set on teaching humans about how anumals behave, interact and socialize. I was the leading technical designer on 7 of them, the complex ones.
