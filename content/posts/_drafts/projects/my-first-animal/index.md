## Overview

My first animal was a joint endeavour of myself and a talented artist. It was made fast with a clear design, to test the market for toddler interaction with clean and simple games. My kids loved it :). In the game, the child can swipe left and right to explore different animal habitats and tap the animals to make them speak. I had a great time working on this, and my kids played it for 2 straight years.
